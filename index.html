About Sophia

Sophia Levy is a lifelong musician and composer who studied at Berklee College of Music and has spent decades creating, producing, and teaching music. A published songwriter and producer, her work has been featured on network television in the U.S. and across Asia. She proudly owned and ran Sophia Music Studio for over a decade, and has now returned passionately to creating music with renewed purpose and heart. Alongside her music career, Sophia has lived a deep wellness journey through yoga, meditation, movement, and mindful living. Today she brings these worlds together, offering sound healing and music meditation experiences that support calm, creativity, and whole-body wellbeing.
